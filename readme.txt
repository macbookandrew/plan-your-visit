Adds the Plan Your Visit code to your WordPress site.

== Description ==

Adds the Plan Your Visit code to your WordPress site without modifying theme files. Enter your ChurchHero.com credentials for one-click installation.

Plan Your Visit is a lead generation tool for churches. Various features such as popups, social proof, and buttons all direct website visitors to a form that allows website visitors to submit their contact information and plan their visit to the church location.

Plan Your Visit is a service of ChurchHero.com, a service to help churches and ministries across the globe enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of their marketing.

== Frequently asked questions ==

= What is Plan Your Visit? =

[Plan Your Visit](https://churchhero.com/) is a lead generation tool for churches. Various features such as popups, social proof, and buttons all direct website visitors to a form that allows website visitors to submit their contact information and plan their visit to the church location.

= What do I need to use this plugin? =

This plugin requires an active ChurchHero.com account as well as WooCommerce.

= What info is sent or received? =

1. When you install the plugin and enter your ChurchHero.com credentials, this plugin will retrieve your account ID.
1. The plugin then installs the Plan Your Visit Javascript code for use on the the frontend of your site.
